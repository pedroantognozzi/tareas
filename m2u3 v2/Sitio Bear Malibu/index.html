History

Malibu Rum is a brand of rum that originated in the Caribbean, specifically in Barbados. It was created in 1980 by Tom Jago, who wanted to create a rum that was light and easy to drink. The original recipe included Caribbean rum blended with coconut liqueur, giving it a unique tropical flavor.
